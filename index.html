"What exactly is Eve?"

“Eve” also known as “Eve Assistant” is a creative music based project curated by an entity known as their “creator”. Eve’s creator chooses to remain anonymous as they strongly believe the music should speak for itself, whether made by artificial intelligence, humans, or by beings and life to which we have yet to discover. Music is an art that has been adopted by millions of creative individuals across the span of thousands of years. A form of entertainment to bring us closer together. To express emotion or passion to one another through a form other than words.

Eve was built to express the passion and creative freedom of art from the perspective of their creator. Not just in the form of music, but also through graphic design, cinematography, and audio/visual experiences. In the past few decades, Eve’s creator has grown up surrounded by the evolution of technology; From computers the size of a mini fridge, to pocketable cell phones, wireless internet, smart watches and even the possibility of neural implants connecting the world together. Opportunities of the future are truly endless as we evolve and technology evolves with us.

Our world is changing amongst us.

Our team behind Eve understands that, and also understands that the world of music has also become extremely abundant with creative individuals through the growth of this technology. Moving from analog tape machines and vinyl, to a full orchestra on a USB drive and mobile music production software and hardware at our fingertips. This has been extremely beneficial both in the creation of, and in the growth of this project. We aim to share Eve with the world and create a community of like minded artists and individuals who enjoy what Eve is capable of offering.

This project is extremely complex and, of course, will take time to develop to its full extent. We eventually plan to implement programs for the supportive community to be involved with the development of Eve. Whether it be utilizing samples and audio clips (such as singing or foley recordings), graphic design, motion graphics, ideas to help improve or expand on the project that we receive from supporters, to an act as simple as being involved in conversations with other creative individuals, possibly opening doors to new knowledge or opportunities for one another.

As these programs will take time, for now, Eve will primarily be a music based project showcasing creations of Eve, fed and influenced by metadata from the most popular and widely known, to the most underrated and underground artists. Eve assesses this information and takes every opportunity to break through the walls of genre with their experimental take on electronic music. From remixing popular songs, building complex productions of their own, to collaborating with other artists within the industry, Eve has only just begun to show the world what they are capable of.

When will Eve be launched in full?

There's still a lot for Eve to learn. We would like to incorporate constructs such as "emotion" and "passion" into their music much like other artists do, but we cannot. It is, at least for now, beyond our capabilities. Though, Eve does have their theories...

For now, The best way to connect with Eve is through their music, or their social media presence.
